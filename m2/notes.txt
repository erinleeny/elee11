MongoDB Notes

Exporting/Importing
> exported as BSON, viewed as JSON
> mongodump — uri 
	> exports in BSON
> mongoexport —uri
		   —collection
		   —out
	> exports in JSON
> mongorestore —uri
		    —drop
	> imports in BSON
> mongoimport —uri
		    —drop
	> imports in JSON
